# DiNC Development Roadmap

**Project:** DiNC (Digital Integrated NCD Care Platform)

**Current Phase:** Phase 2 – Functional Development

# Vision

Build a modern, scalable healthcare operations platform for Non-Communicable Disease (NCD) management that supports patient enrollment, clinical workflows, guidebooks, activities, reviews, reporting, and administration.
